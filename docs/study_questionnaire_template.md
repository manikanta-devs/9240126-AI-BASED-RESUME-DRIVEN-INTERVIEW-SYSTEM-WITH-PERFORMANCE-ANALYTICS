> [!TIP]
> **How to calculate the SUS Score:**
> - For odd-numbered questions (1, 3, 5, 7, 9): subtract 1 from the score (e.g. if answer is 4, score is 3).
> - For even-numbered questions (2, 4, 6, 8, 10): subtract the score from 5 (e.g. if answer is 2, score is 3).
> - Add up the individual scores and multiply the total by **2.5**. This gives you a score out of 100.
> - **Top-tier target**: A mean score of **80.0 or higher** indicates Excellent Usability.
